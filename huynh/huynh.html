Reviews

"Over a series of visits to this family-run venture, I encountered one impressive dish after another. I grew to love the chic-on-a-shoestring look of the pretty dining room, done up by the Huynh siblings in soft green and warm persimmon hues, with sleekly curved contemporary chairs and restrained lighting. Dishes marked with a smiley face on the menu — to indicate house specialties — actually made me smile. Imagine that." -Houston Chronicles Revviews
